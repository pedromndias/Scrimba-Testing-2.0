Visit Oeiras

Enjoy culture, sports and sun in this vibrant town close to Lisbon.

Top three activities to do at Oeiras

Go to the beach

Enjoy Oeiras's sunny beaches, bathed by the mighty Atlantlic Ocean.

Visit a park

Enjoy Oeiras's large Parque dos Poetas, born to honor poets and poetry.

Enjoy sports

Attend Estoril Open, the tournament for category ATP World Tour 250 held in town.
